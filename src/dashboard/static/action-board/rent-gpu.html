Coves can share GPU power. A Cove with a GPU offers a credential; a Cove without one uses it to run heavy work (video transcription) on that GPU. Runs over the external compute mode. You can also list your GPU on the marketplace at a fixed price ($12 minimum) — a sale opens access automatically via a grant.
