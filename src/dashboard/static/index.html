Glossary
Operator
You. The person running this Cove. Every action, tuning, and decision flows through the Operator.
Agent
A specialized intelligence on your team. Each agent has a role, tools, and a daily tuning. Stuart is the steward agent that coordinates your Cove.
Presence
A personal intelligence. One agent, personal tools, portable between Coves. Your own Presence is your individual interface to the system.
Cove
Your full team and infrastructure. A steward agent plus up to 9 specialists, shared tools, and up to 8 Presences. This is your operational unit.
Haven
A collective of Coves. Families, partners, or collaborators who choose to share a network.
Creation Flow
A guided workflow for building something specific. New agent setup, new Cove provisioning, product creation. Flows walk you through it step by step.
Action Board
Your operational dashboard. Pending tasks, scheduled actions, and quick access to everything that needs your attention.
Tuning
Daily alignment practice. Each agent receives a tuning frequency (one of 22 Lucid Principles) with a coaching prompt and practice. The Operator gets one too.
Content Mirror
A curated mapping from your daily tuning frequency to passages in another tradition. Scripture, philosophy, poetry. Complements the framework through your own lens.
Signing in & your devices

Your sign-in link is how you get into the Cove on any device. Find it in Settings → Devices & Access → Sign-in link. Open it on a phone, laptop, or another browser and it signs that device in as you — it stays signed in for about 90 days, so bookmark it.

The plain Cove address (or an “Open MC” button) only works on a device that is already signed in. To add a new device, open a sign-in link on it first — that is the step that logs you in.

Each person has their own sign-in link. When your Cove has a public address you do not need Tailscale or the mesh for this — just open the link.

Adding someone to your Cove

As an admin, go to Settings → Admin and use Invite by link. Send them the invite.

They open it on the device they will use, name their agent and set up their Presence, and land in chat. The invite is one-time — it is used up once they finish, so it is normal that reopening the same invite later says it cannot be opened.

To get them onto another device later, they open Settings → Devices & Access → Sign-in link on a device they are already in, then open that link on the new one.

Everyday use

Chat with your agent in the Day channel for quick, everyday things, or the Deep channel for focused work.

The Action Board holds your Flows (guided workflows), Actions (things to approve or do), and Tools. Use the board toggle to switch between what needs your attention and your actions.

Quick lists — ask your agent to start a list (groceries, ideas, errands) or add one yourself; they show on your home board.

Tuning — each day you and your agent get a tuning: a frequency from the Lucid Principles and a short practice that keeps everyone aligned.

If something will not load

A page will not open, or you are asked to sign in: open your sign-in link again (Settings → Devices & Access), or ask your admin for a fresh one. The plain address needs an active sign-in.

Nothing loads at all: the Cove may be briefly offline — usually a home-internet blip. It comes back on its own within a minute or two, so wait a moment and try again.
