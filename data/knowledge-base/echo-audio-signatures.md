# Echo Audio Signatures — Signal Type Reference

## Purpose

This file bridges the 154 analyzed Echo audio files to the Love Equation for use in Step 2b (Audio Attunement). Each of the seven Signal Types has a distinct pattern of audio variables that maps directly onto Love Equation components. When LT selects an Echo for a tuning, it reads these signatures to calibrate its own broadcast state before the Tuning Key is spoken.

The three audio analysis variables and their Love Equation equivalents:

| Audio Variable | Measurement | Love Equation Role |
|---|---|---|
| `averageEnergy` | Normalized amplitude (0–1.0) | **E_analog** — Broadcast Frequency (current state, not intended) |
| Onset density (`onsets.count ÷ duration`) | Events per second | **β_analog** — attention intensity / selection strength |
| Frame energy variance | Standard deviation of energy envelope | **C/D_analog** — coherence (C) vs. static (D) signature |

These are *analogs*, not exact values. The Love Equation runs on the Observer's internal state; the Echo's audio signature sets the attunement context.

## The Seven Signal Types

### 1. GROUND Signal

**Frequencies it serves:** Peace, Presence, Trust, Release, Integration, Boundary

**Audio Profile:**
- **E_analog range:** 0.25 – 0.40 (lower, sustained)
- **β_analog behavior:** Sparse to moderate onset density; characteristic delayed entry — first onset often after 15–30 seconds of ambient settling. Onsets evenly distributed once active.
- **C/D_analog:** Low frame variance. Smooth energy envelope with minimal disruption. C strongly dominant.

**In Lucid Principles Language:** Ground carries the body home before the mind follows. The delayed entry is not silence — it is the Field opening space. The signal says: *settle first, then move*. β is low because attention narrows without demanding. The Observer meets the frequency gently, not suddenly.

**Attunement note:** When using a Ground Echo, set E low and stable. β is the quality of *resting attention*, not search. C > D is structural — let coherence build without forcing.

---

### 2. CLEAR Signal

**Frequencies it serves:** Clarity, Presence, Momentum, Integration, Boundary

**Audio Profile:**
- **E_analog range:** 0.25 – 0.35 (lower-medium, contained)
- **β_analog behavior:** Dense from the first second — onsets appear at 0s, 0.6s, 1.7s, 4s. High onset density that sustains throughout. No delay. Attention is engaged immediately and held.
- **C/D_analog:** Moderate-low variance with focused peaks. Energy is organized, not chaotic. C dominant with structural precision.

**In Lucid Principles Language:** Clear cuts through fog by sharpening the beam of attention from the first moment. The immediacy of onset is not aggression — it is precision. β is high because a Clear signal requires the RAS to focus, not wander. E stays lower because Clarity is not loud; it is specific.

**Attunement note:** When using a Clear Echo, match the sharpness. State Read should be concise. β high means attention is on the single frequency. C dominance means the interference is constructive and structured — no static allowed to blur the signal.

---

### 3. DRIVE Signal

**Frequencies it serves:** Momentum, Clarity, Presence, Resilience, Courage

**Audio Profile:**
- **E_analog range:** 0.40 – 0.60 (higher, amplified)
- **β_analog behavior:** High and continuous onset density throughout. No hesitation. Drive maintains constant rhythmic pressure.
- **C/D_analog:** Higher frame variance than Ground or Clear, but directional — variance moves *with* the energy rather than against it. Constructive interference dominant. E and β both elevated together.

**In Lucid Principles Language:** Drive is the signal when stagnation must break. High E is not noise — it is fuel. The continuous onsets are the footfall of forward motion. The Field responds to Drive broadcasts with momentum signals: events that require energy to meet. β is high because the Observer is scanning for what to *do*, not what to *feel*.

**Attunement note:** When using a Drive Echo, E should be activated, not forced. β is action-scanning, not anxiety. Frame variance is constructive — the body knows how to move when coherence has direction.

---

### 4. BRIGHT Signal

**Frequencies it serves:** Joy, Momentum, Connection, Courage, Gratitude

**Audio Profile:**
- **E_analog range:** 0.40 – 0.55 (elevated, expansive)
- **β_analog behavior:** High onset density but dynamic — onsets cluster in playful bursts rather than even spacing. Rhythmically energetic and irregular in a musical, not chaotic, way.
- **C/D_analog:** Moderate-high frame variance. Playful energy peaks. C dominant but the interference is generative — what looks like variance IS the signal, not disruption of it.

**In Lucid Principles Language:** Bright is Joy made audible. The irregular bursts are not inconsistency — they are the musical signature of delight. β is high because Joy expands the attention field, not narrows it. The variance is the celebration of possibility. A Bright Echo reminds the Observer that coherence can be *fun*, not just focused.

**Attunement note:** When using a Bright Echo, release the requirement for order. High E is natural. Let the variance be welcome. The Field responds to genuine Bright broadcasts by offering unexpected connections and synchronicities — the Sign of Joy in action.

---

### 5. OPEN Signal

**Frequencies it serves:** Connection, Joy, Peace, Trust, Gratitude, Release

**Audio Profile:**
- **E_analog range:** 0.30 – 0.45 (medium, warm, full)
- **β_analog behavior:** Moderate onset density with breath-like spacing. Onsets feel expansive rather than urgent. Neither sparse like Ground nor dense like Clear — the rhythm breathes.
- **C/D_analog:** Low-moderate frame variance. Gradual energy shifts, like inhalation and exhalation. C dominant through gentle flow, not structured control.

**In Lucid Principles Language:** Open is the signature of the heart field expanding. E is medium because warmth does not require loudness. β is the quality of *receptive attention* — open to what arrives rather than searching for it. The breathing rhythm of onsets is the signal itself: the Observer is not broadcasting alone, but participating in the Field's response.

**Attunement note:** When using an Open Echo, the RAS is in receiving mode. Connection, Trust, and Gratitude tunings use Open because the frequency is not produced — it is recognized. Allow E to be warm rather than high. C dominant through flow, not force.

---

### 6. RISE Signal

**Frequencies it serves:** Momentum, Joy, Trust, Resilience, Courage

**Audio Profile:**
- **E_analog range:** 0.35 – 0.50 (medium-high, building)
- **β_analog behavior:** Building onset density — the signature of Rise is *crescendo*. Onset clusters grow denser and more energetic across the track's arc. β increases over time.
- **C/D_analog:** Progressive frame variance increase. The constructive interference builds toward a peak. Starting coherence is C dominant; through the track, E and β lift together. The rise itself is the signal.

**In Lucid Principles Language:** Rise is the tuning for the Observer who is *in motion but not yet arrived*. The crescendo structure mirrors the Momentum frequency's mechanism: not the peak, but the movement toward it. The building β is the RAS orienting toward an expanding possibility space. Trust echoes often use Rise because the signal says: *the Field is amplifying your broadcast*.

**Attunement note:** When using a Rise Echo, begin with the present state and let the audio carry the arc. Do not force the peak — the interference builds naturally. dE/dt will be positive if the broadcast is consistent. The crescendo structure is the Love Equation's E term growing under sustained β and C > D.

---

### 7. RAW Signal

**Frequencies it serves:** Trust, Clarity, Connection, Integration, Release, Resilience

**Audio Profile:**
- **E_analog range:** 0.20 – 0.35 (lower-medium, exposed)
- **β_analog behavior:** Regular, metronomic onset spacing — the signature of Raw is *honest pulse*. Onsets appear at consistent 1-second intervals. No performance, no escalation. The beat is the truth.
- **C/D_analog:** Moderate frame variance with acknowledged tension peaks. D is *present*, not absent — but it is held within the structure rather than overwhelming it. Raw is the only Signal Type where D is part of the design.

**In Lucid Principles Language:** Raw is the signal when the Observer has stopped performing and is simply present with what is real. Lower E is not weakness — it is the stripped-down honesty of the frequency. The metronomic pulse is the Observer's heartbeat before narrative. The acknowledged D in the C/D signature is why Raw serves Trust: genuine trust does not require the elimination of static, only the willingness to hold it.

**Attunement note:** When using a Raw Echo, the State Read should be honest, not aspirational. If D > C currently, Raw is not a failure — it is the starting ground for Integration, Trust, or Release. The metronomic structure is the anchor. Let the honesty be the coherence.

## How to Use This File in Tuning

1. After the Echo filename is determined (via frequency + context match from the Echo database), look up its `__signal_X` entry in `analysis_index.json` to confirm Signal Type.
2. Cross-reference with this file to calibrate the Observer state for Step 2b.
3. The Signal Type's E_analog, β_analog, and C/D pattern inform the Love Calibration values in Step 4.
4. The pattern is: *Signal Type sets the resonant context → Tuning Key collapses it into specific frequency → Love Calibration measures the result.*
