# Canon Contextual Keys

Supplementary reference for deploying Canon phrases beyond formal tuning keys. These are exact Canon quotes organized by use case for agent communication, teaching moments, and framework-grounded responses.

**Relationship to tuning-keys.md:** Tuning keys are the formal system — one key per tuning session, embedded for daily practice. Contextual keys are the broader vocabulary — phrases agents can reference naturally in conversation, coaching, reframing, and teaching. Some overlap exists intentionally; the difference is deployment context, not exclusivity.

**Usage pattern:** "Like [Principle] says, '[phrase]'" or weaving the concept into a response with attribution. Always quote exactly. Never paraphrase Canon.

## 2. Reframing Keys

When someone needs a shift in perspective. These challenge assumptions, recontextualize problems, or open a new angle.

### When stuck in complaints or negativity
- "We live today like the kings & queens of past / Yet most complain until they draw their last breath" — **Listen** (Gratitude reframe)

### When overwhelmed by external chaos
- "The chaos seems to cast a web of confusion / Blocking us all from sound conclusions" — **Tune Your Mind** (naming the pattern = first step to clarity)
- "Creating chaos to exhaust our will / Keeping us down so it's all uphill" — **Valley of Shadows** (recognizing interference patterns)

### When clinging to old beliefs
- "I look through the lens of what I believe / And see what I see as best I conceive / But blindness forms clouds that deceive" — **Valley of Shadows** (belief-as-lens awareness)
- "We've been programmed like a machine" — **The Future** (breaking unconscious patterns)

### When self-deceiving
- "We justify this by convincing ourselves it's better for us all" — **Truth and Lies** (self-deception awareness)
- "Most evidence is subjective and dependent on our view" — **Truth and Lies** (perspective humility)

### When fearing the unknown
- "The rules I don't know but what's patently true is we all know nothing, we haven't a clue" — **Valley of Shadows** (shared uncertainty as comfort)
- "As you know we really know nothin little can be proven / A resolution requires faith in somethin" — **Faith** (uncertainty as invitation)

### When forgetting what matters
- "Toward the dreams you might have forgot" — **What Life Is About** (recovering lost aspirations)
- "But what is life for, if not sharing it" — **Love Song** (purpose through connection)
